# DESIGN.md

## Design Intent
Clean business narrative with high-contrast typography and predictable content zones.

## Canvas
- Slide size: 13.333in x 7.5in
- Aspect ratio: 1.778

## Color System
- Candidate palette: #FFFFFF, #111111, #0A84FF, #DDE3F0
- Neutrals: #FFFFFF, #111111
- Primary: #0A84FF
- Accents: #DDE3F0

## Typography
- Fonts: Calibri, Arial
- Size rhythm: 36, 24, 18, 14, 10
- Roles: title, subtitle, body, caption

## Spacing
- Left anchors: ~1.0in, ~1.5in
- Top anchors: ~0.7in, ~1.6in
- Vertical gap rhythm: ~0.25in to ~0.5in

## Shapes and Components
- Repeating title block at upper-left anchor
- Metric card containers in consistent grid widths
- Footer note in lower region with compact text

## Layout Archetypes
- cover slide
- title + two-column
- image + text
- metrics/cards

## Composition Rules
- Keep title within top third and left anchor.
- Preserve two-column rhythm for comparison slides.
- Reuse component sizes before introducing new proportions.

## AI Generation Guidance
- Pick one archetype and fill content while preserving spacing and hierarchy.
- Use observed fonts/colors first when uncertain.
